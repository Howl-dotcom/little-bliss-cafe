About us

Celebrating life and its wonders.

Little Bliss is a small cakes-and-coffee corner in Bato, Catanduanes — a place to slow down over a good cup, celebrate the small stuff, and catch up with people who matter. Whether you're dropping by for a quiet coffee or picking up a cake for someone's big day, we try to make every visit feel a little sweeter.
